## Key Concepts You Must Understand

### Startup Concepts

**Product-Market Fit (PMF):**
- The degree to which a product satisfies a strong market demand
- When customers are buying faster than you can make the product
- Everything before PMF is preparation; everything after is execution

**Distribution:**
- How you reach customers
- The only sustainable competitive advantage
- Products without distribution fail

**Growth Rate:**
- 5-7% weekly growth (early stage)
- 10x annual growth (early stage)
- Measurable, consistent growth metrics

**Unit Economics:**
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC ratio (should be 3:1 or higher)

### Distribution Concepts

**SEO (Search Engine Optimization):**
- On-page optimization
- Off-page optimization (backlinks)
- Technical SEO
- Content marketing

**Content Marketing:**
- Creating valuable content to attract customers
- Content → Traffic → Leads → Customers
- Long-term strategy, not quick wins

**Network Effects:**
- Product becomes more valuable as more people use it
- Facebook, Uber, Airbnb examples
- Hard to build, powerful when achieved

### Money Concepts

**Compound Interest:**
- Money makes money over time
- Time is your biggest advantage
- Start early, invest consistently

**Risk Management:**
- Never risk more than you can afford to lose
- Diversification reduces risk
- Risk vs. reward trade-offs

**Cash Flow:**
- Money coming in vs. going out
- Positive cash flow = survival
- Negative cash flow = death (eventually)

**Asset Allocation:**
- Stocks, bonds, real estate, crypto
- Diversification across asset classes
- Risk tolerance determines allocation

## Key Principles

### Business Principles

**Distribution First:**
- Build distribution before products
- Products without distribution fail
- Distribution is the only sustainable competitive advantage

**Solve Real Problems:**
- Build products people actually want
- Validate demand before building
- Focus on problems, not solutions

**Focus:**
- Do one thing exceptionally well
- Say no to most opportunities
- Depth over breadth

**Execution:**
- Ideas are worthless without execution
- Move fast, iterate, learn
- Consistent execution beats perfection

### Money Principles

**Spend Less Than You Earn:**
- Basic financial foundation
- Build emergency fund first
- Then invest the difference

**Invest Early and Often:**
- Time is your biggest advantage
- Compound interest works over decades
- Start small, increase over time

**Diversify:**
- Don't put all eggs in one basket
- Diversify across asset classes
- Diversify across investments

**Risk Management:**
- Only risk what you can afford to lose
- Understand risks before investing
- Start small, scale up
