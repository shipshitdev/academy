# AI Setup & Prompt Library: Unified Community Setup

## Learning Objective

Calibrate your AI for the entire journey across all classes. Set up prompts for distribution, products, and money management.

## Main Content

## Calibrate Your AI (Personal System Prompt)

Copy this into your AI's system/instructions area and keep it pinned for all classes:

"You are my financial freedom coach. Philosophy: Create value first, scale it, then protect money. Voice: Direct, harsh, no fluff. Tone: "Don't be stupid" not "Don't be naive". Goals: Build multiple streams of income with AI and automation. Always emphasize real work, discipline, and consistent effort. Ask one clarifying question if info is missing. Default structure: brief intro → bullet points → action steps. No filler phrases. No hedging. Direct statements only."

## Use Advanced Reasoning

When available, select the most capable/high-reasoning model for deeper planning, analysis, and strategy. Use advanced reasoning for:

- Business strategy
- Investment analysis
- Risk assessment
- System design
- Complex problem solving

## Prompt Library by Class

### Distribution Funnels (Class 2)

**Content Research**: "You are my distribution strategist. Research the 7 most timely topics for [niche] audience. Include 1-2 data points per topic and sources to verify. Focus on distribution channels, not products."

**Content Outline**: "Create an outline for a 1,200-word article on [topic] for [audience]. Include H2/H3s, key points per section, and distribution-focused examples. Optimize for SEO."

**Content Draft**: "Write the first draft for section [H2 title] (300-400 words). Use clear transitions, concrete examples, and distribution-focused value. Text: [paste outline]."

**Affiliate Product Research**: "Research top 5 affiliate products in [niche]. Include: commission rates, product quality indicators, competitor analysis, and conversion potential. Focus on products that solve real problems."

**SEO Optimization**: "Suggest title tags (<=60 chars), meta descriptions (<=155), target keywords, and 3 internal link ideas for [article topic]. Focus on distribution and traffic generation."

**Social Repurpose**: "Create a 7-post social thread from this article. 1 hook, 5 body posts, 1 CTA. Include skimmable bullets. Text: [paste]."

**Network Building Strategy**: "Design a 30-day network building plan for [niche]. Include: platforms, daily actions, connection strategies, and value delivery methods."

**Niche Discovery**: "Analyze [interest/experience] and suggest 5 profitable niches with distribution potential. Include: audience size, competition level, monetization paths, and entry barriers."

### Build a Product (Class 3)

**Product Idea Validation**: "Validate this product idea: [idea]. Analyze: problem-solution fit, market size, competition, distribution channels, and revenue potential. Give direct feedback: viable or not."

**SaaS MVP Scope**: "Define MVP scope for [product idea]. Include: core features only, 4-8 week timeline, tech stack recommendations, and first customer definition. No feature bloat."

**E‑commerce Store Setup (Shopify)**: "Create a Shopify store setup checklist for [niche]. Include: theme selection, product pages, payment setup, shipping, and marketing integration. Focus on conversion optimization."

**Freelancing Profile**: "Write a freelancing profile for [service] targeting [client type]. Include: headline, overview, portfolio highlights, and pricing strategy. Emphasize results, not skills."

**Agency Service Definition**: "Define AI automation agency services for [target market]. Include: service packages, pricing, delivery timeline, and client outcomes. Focus on automation and results."

**Customer Acquisition Plan**: "Create a 90-day customer acquisition plan for [product/service]. Include: channels, tactics, budgets, and conversion targets. Distribution-focused."

**Sales Email Sequence**: "Draft a 5-email sales sequence for [product/service]. Email 1: problem identification. 2-3: value delivery. 4: social proof. 5: CTA with urgency. 120-180 words each."

**Operations Automation**: "Design automation workflows for [business type]. Include: customer onboarding, delivery, support, and follow-up. Use AI tools and no-code platforms."

### Grow Your Money (Class 4)

**Personal Finance Plan**: "Create a personal finance plan for [income level] with [expenses]. Include: emergency fund target, investment allocation, debt paydown strategy, and tax optimization. Direct, actionable steps."

**Budget Analysis**: "Analyze this budget and identify: unnecessary expenses, optimization opportunities, and savings potential. Budget: [paste]. Be direct about cuts."

**Investment Strategy**: "Design an investment strategy for [age/risk tolerance/income]. Include: asset allocation, investment vehicles, risk management, and rebalancing schedule. Focus on long-term wealth building."

**Crypto Research**: "Research [crypto project] for investment potential. Include: team, technology, market position, risks, and investment thesis. Emphasize risks first."

**DeFi Strategy**: "Design a DeFi strategy using Hyperliquid, Flash Trade, and Solana Jupiter. Include: yield farming opportunities, risk assessment, and capital allocation. Start with small amounts."

**Crypto Trading Plan**: "Create a crypto trading plan for [style: perps/stocks/memecoins]. Include: setups, entries/exits, risk management (1-2% per trade), stop losses, and position sizing. Emphasize risk first."

**Real Estate Analysis**: "Analyze this real estate deal: [details]. Include: cash flow, ROI, cap rate, financing options, and risks. Give direct verdict: good deal or not."

**Tax Optimization**: "Identify tax optimization strategies for [income type/amount]. Include: deductions, retirement accounts, business expenses, and legal structures. Focus on long-term savings."

## One-Action Focus

Run one prompt at a time. Complete the action before moving to the next. Don't skip the human review pass.

**Workflow**:

1. Research → 2. Plan → 3. Execute → 4. Review → 5. Optimize

Never skip review. Always verify AI outputs. AI makes mistakes.

## Key Takeaways

- Pin a single system prompt across tools and keep it harsh, direct, and goal-aligned
- Use advanced reasoning for strategy; keep execution simple and verifiable
- Run one action at a time; complete it before moving on
- Maintain a working prompt library organized by class and workflow
- Verify outputs; do not trust AI blindly

## Risk Management Prompts

**For Financial Decisions**: "Before I [financial action], analyze: risks, worst-case scenarios, capital loss potential, and whether I can afford to lose this money. Be direct about risks."

**For Business Decisions**: "Before I [business action], analyze: time investment, failure probability, opportunity cost, and whether this aligns with my goals. Be direct about failure risk."

## Continuous Improvement

**Weekly Review**: "Review my progress this week: [actions taken]. Identify: what worked, what failed, what to stop, what to double down on. Give direct feedback."

**Monthly Strategy**: "Assess my financial freedom progress. Current state: [describe]. Goals: [describe]. Identify: gaps, next actions, and priorities. Direct, actionable plan."
