# Scope and Overview: What You'll Learn

## Learning Objective

Understand what you'll learn across all four classes and how they build the complete journey from zero to financial freedom.

## Main Content

## The Four Classes

This community has four classes that build the complete journey:

1. **Getting Started** (this class) - Philosophy, disclaimers, overview, AI setup
2. **Build Distribution Funnels** - Distribution channels, content creation, affiliate marketing
3. **Build a Product** - SaaS, E‑commerce (Shopify), freelancing, agencies, indie dev
4. **Grow Your Money** - Personal finance, crypto, DeFi, trading, real estate

Follow them in order. Each class builds on the previous one.

## Class 1: Getting Started

**Purpose**: Foundation. Philosophy, disclaimers, scope, AI setup.

**What You'll Learn**:

- The philosophy: Create value first, scale, then protect
- Comprehensive disclaimers and risks
- Overview of all classes
- How to set up AI tools for the entire journey

**Modules**: 4 modules
**Time Required**: 1-2 hours
**Prerequisites**: None

## Class 2: Build Distribution Funnels

**Purpose**: Build distribution channels first. Distribution before products.

**What You'll Learn**:

- Why distribution comes first
- How to use AI for content creation at scale
- How to build affiliate marketing businesses
- How to build your network
- How to discover your niche fast

**Modules**: ~20+ modules (content creation + affiliate marketing)
**Time Required**: 20-40 hours
**Prerequisites**: Getting Started class

**Key Skills**:

- AI-powered content creation
- SEO optimization
- Affiliate marketing
- Network building
- Niche discovery

**Outcome**: You'll have distribution channels and an audience before building products.

## Class 3: Build a Product

**Purpose**: Build products that generate income. Multiple product types.

**What You'll Learn**:

- How to build indie dev systems
- How to build SaaS products (0 to $10k MRR)
- How to build e‑commerce shops (dropshipping; Shopify)
- How to build freelancing businesses
- How to build AI automation agencies

**Modules**: ~50+ modules (indie-dev + startup + dropshipping + freelancing + agencies)
**Time Required**: 50-100 hours
**Prerequisites**: Distribution Funnels class (or at least started)

**Key Skills**:

- Product development
- Marketing and sales
- Customer acquisition
- Operations and automation
- Scaling systems

**Outcome**: You'll have income-generating products and systems.

## Class 4: Grow Your Money

**Purpose**: Protect and grow your capital. Multiple investment strategies.

**What You'll Learn**:

- Personal finance fundamentals
- Economics basics
- Crypto and DeFi (Hyperliquid, Flash Trade, Solana Jupiter)
- Crypto trading (perps, stocks, memecoins)
- Real estate investing

**Modules**: ~40+ modules (personal finance + crypto + trading + real estate)
**Time Required**: 40-80 hours
**Prerequisites**: You need income to protect and grow

**Key Skills**:

- Money management
- Investment strategies
- Risk management
- Crypto trading
- Real estate analysis

**Outcome**: You'll protect and grow your capital across multiple asset classes.

## The Complete Journey

**From Zero to Financial Freedom**:

1. **Start**: No distribution, no products, no capital
2. **Build Distribution**: Create channels, build audience, discover niche
3. **Build Products**: Create income-generating systems
4. **Protect and Grow**: Manage money, invest, build wealth

Each step uses AI. Each step builds automation. Each step moves you toward financial freedom.

## Time Investment

**Total Time**: 100-200+ hours across all classes

**Realistic Timeline**:

- Getting Started: 1 day
- Distribution Funnels: 1-2 months
- Build a Product: 3-6 months
- Grow Your Money: Ongoing

**Most people won't complete all classes**. That's fine. Focus on what you need. Complete what matters to you.

## Project-Based Learning

Each class includes hands-on projects:

- **Distribution Funnels**: Build content systems, create affiliate sites
- **Build a Product**: Build actual products, get first customers
- **Grow Your Money**: Create financial plans, make first investments

Projects are required. Theory without practice doesn't work.

## AI Integration

Every class uses AI:

- AI for content creation
- AI for product development
- AI for market analysis
- AI for investment research

AI accelerates everything. AI doesn't replace work. You still need to do the work.

## Multiple Streams of Income

This community teaches multiple streams:

1. **Distribution Income**: Affiliate marketing, content monetization
2. **Product Income**: SaaS, E‑commerce, freelancing, agencies
3. **Investment Income**: Crypto, DeFi, trading, real estate

Each stream requires different skills. Each stream can fail. Build multiple streams.
