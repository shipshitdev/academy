# Veo3 Video Prompt: Scope and Overview: What You'll Learn

## Video Specifications
- **Type**: Facecam (person speaking directly to camera)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds maximum
- **Style**: Direct, confident, no-nonsense delivery
- **Setting**: Professional, clean background, good lighting

## Script

In this module, you'll learn about what you'll learn. You'll get practical steps you can use immediately. This takes work. No shortcuts.

## Delivery Notes
- Speak directly to camera
- Confident, direct tone
- No fluff or filler words
- Maintain eye contact
- Natural gestures are okay but keep it minimal
- End with clear, actionable statement

## Visual Elements
- Close-up to medium shot (head and shoulders)
- Person looking directly at camera
- Professional appearance
- Clean, uncluttered background
