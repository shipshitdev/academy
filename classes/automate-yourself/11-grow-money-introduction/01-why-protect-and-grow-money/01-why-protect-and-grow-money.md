# Why Protect and Grow Your Money

## Learning Objective

Understand why you protect and grow money after building income streams. Learn how to protect what you've built and grow your capital.

## Main Content

## Why Protect and Grow Money After Income

**Income First**: You built distribution (Classes 2-4). You built products (Classes 5-10). You have income streams.

**Protect What You've Built**: After you have income, protect it. Don't lose what you've earned. Build emergency funds. Manage money wisely.

**Grow Your Capital**: After you protect it, grow it. Invest wisely. Build wealth. Multiple streams of investment income.

**You Need Income First**: Don't skip income building. You need money to protect and grow. Build income streams first.

## Multiple Investment Strategies

**Personal Finance Fundamentals**: Track money, budget, invest first. Foundation for everything.

**Crypto and DeFi**: High risk, high reward. Use Hyperliquid, Flash Trade, Solana Jupiter. Understand the risks.

**Crypto Trading**: Highest risk, highest reward. Trade perps, stocks, memecoins. You can lose everything.

**Real Estate**: Lower risk, steady growth. Analyze deals, manage properties, scale portfolios.

## Risk Levels

**Low Risk**: Personal finance, real estate. Steady growth. Lower returns.

**High Risk**: Crypto, DeFi, crypto trading. Volatile markets. Higher returns possible. Capital loss possible.

Understand the risks. Match risk to your situation. Start with low risk, add high risk as you learn.

## Key Takeaways

- Protect and grow money after you have income
- Personal finance is the foundation
- Crypto and DeFi are high risk
- Crypto trading is highest risk
- Real estate is lower risk
- Diversify investments
- You can lose everything in crypto
- Start small, learn properly
