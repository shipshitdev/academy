## Main Content

Setting up a wallet is your first step into cryptocurrency. Let's do it step-by-step:

**Choosing Your Wallet Type**:

For this project, we'll start with a software wallet (easiest for beginners):
- **MetaMask**: Most popular, works with DeFi and NFTs
- **Exodus**: User-friendly, good for beginners
- **Coinbase Wallet**: Simple, from trusted exchange

We'll use MetaMask as example (most versatile), but process is similar for others.

**Step 1: Download MetaMask**

- Go to metamask.io (official site only—beware fake sites)
- Download browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Edge)
- Or download mobile app (iOS/Android)
- Never download from third-party sites

**Step 2: Create Your Wallet**

- Click "Create a Wallet"
- Create a strong password (write it down securely)
- Read and accept terms (understand what you're agreeing to)
- You'll be shown your seed phrase next

**Step 3: Save Your Seed Phrase (CRITICAL)**

**What is a Seed Phrase?**
- 12 or 24 random words that can recover your wallet
- If you lose this, you lose access forever
- If someone gets this, they can steal everything

**How to Save It**:
- Write it down on paper (never digitally)
- Store in secure, fireproof location
- Make backup copy (store separately)
- Never share with anyone, ever
- No screenshots, no cloud storage, no emails

**Step 4: Verify Your Seed Phrase**

- MetaMask will ask you to verify by selecting words in order
- This ensures you saved it correctly
- Complete this step carefully

**Step 5: Explore Your Wallet**

**Your Wallet Contains**:
- **Account Address**: Public address (like account number). Safe to share.
- **Balance**: Shows your cryptocurrency (starts at zero)
- **Networks**: Different blockchains (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
- **Send/Receive**: Buttons to transfer crypto

**Step 6: Get Your Public Address**

- Click "Account" to copy your address
- This is what you share to receive cryptocurrency
- Looks like: 0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b8...

**Step 7: Test with Small Amount**

**Practice Receiving** (if you have crypto elsewhere):
- Copy your MetaMask address
- Send small test amount from exchange
- Wait for confirmation (can take minutes)
- Verify it appears in wallet

**Or Practice with Testnet**:
- Switch to Goerli testnet (Ethereum test network)
- Get free test ETH from faucet
- Practice sending/receiving with no risk

**Step 8: Security Hardening**

**Enable Additional Security**:
- Set up password for transactions (if available)
- Enable biometric lock (mobile)
- Review connected sites regularly
- Never share seed phrase

**Step 9: Understand What You Control**

**You Now Control**:
- Your private keys (via seed phrase)
- Your cryptocurrency (when you add it)
- Your transactions (you approve each one)

**You're Responsible For**:
- Keeping seed phrase safe
- Not falling for scams
- Verifying addresses before sending
- Understanding what you're doing

**Step 10: Learn Wallet Basics**

**Sending Cryptocurrency**:
- Click "Send"
- Paste recipient address (double-check!)
- Enter amount
- Review gas fees
- Confirm transaction
- Wait for confirmation

**Receiving Cryptocurrency**:
- Share your public address (or QR code)
- Sender sends to that address
- Appears in wallet after confirmation

**Common Mistakes to Avoid**:
- Not saving seed phrase (lose access)
- Saving seed phrase digitally (security risk)
- Sending to wrong address (permanent loss)
- Not verifying addresses before sending
- Sharing seed phrase (scam risk)
- Downloading from fake sites

**Next Steps After Setup**:

**Practice**:
- Send small test transaction to yourself (if you have crypto)
- Explore wallet interface
- Learn how to switch networks
- Understand gas fees

**Education**:
- Learn about different networks (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
- Understand gas fees and how they work
- Research security best practices
- Learn to identify scams

**Your Wallet is Now Ready**:
- You have a secure place to store cryptocurrency
- You control your private keys
- You can send and receive crypto
- You're ready for next steps (buying crypto, using DeFi, etc.)

**Remember**:
- Your seed phrase is your wallet—protect it
- Start with small amounts to practice
- Double-check everything before sending
- Never share private keys or seed phrase
- This is your responsibility—there's no customer service to recover lost funds

## Key Takeaways
- Download wallet from official site only (metamask.io)
- Seed phrase is critical—write it down on paper, never digitally, never share
- Practice with small amounts or testnet before using real money
- You're responsible for security—no one can recover lost seed phrases
- Double-check all addresses before sending—transactions are irreversible
