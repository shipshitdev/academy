# Project 4: Systematize and Scale

## Learning Objective

Systematize your AI automation agency operations and scale to multiple clients and sustainable income.

## Main Content

This project will walk you through systematizing your agency and scaling to multiple clients. This is a 3-6 month journey to sustainable agency operations.

**The Scaling Timeline:**

**Months 1-2: Systematize**
- Document all processes
- Create templates
- Build delivery systems
- Establish workflows
- 1-2 clients

**Months 3-4: Scale**
- Increase client acquisition
- Improve delivery efficiency
- Build team (optional)
- Optimize processes
- 3-5 clients

**Months 5-6: Optimize**
- Refine systems
- Focus on high-value clients
- Scale operations
- Build recurring revenue
- 5-10+ clients
