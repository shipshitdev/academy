## Main Content

## Why Distribution Comes First

**The Reality**: Products without distribution fail. You can build the best product in the world, but if no one knows about it, it fails.

**Distribution is the bottleneck**. Not product quality. Not features. Distribution.

**Build distribution channels now**. Before you build products. Before you spend money on products. Build distribution first.

## The Philosophy: Distribution Before Products

**Step 1**: Build distribution channels

- Create content
- Build audience
- Grow network
- Establish authority

**Step 2**: Then build products

- Products for your audience
- Products your network wants
- Products that solve real problems
- Products with built-in distribution

**Don't skip Step 1**. Most people skip it. Most people fail.
