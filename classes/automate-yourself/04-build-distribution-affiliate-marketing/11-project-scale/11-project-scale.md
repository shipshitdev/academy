# Project 4: Launch and Scale Your Affiliate Business

## Learning Objective

Launch your affiliate marketing business and scale it to sustainable income through content creation, SEO optimization, and conversion improvement.

## Main Content

This project will walk you through launching and scaling your affiliate business. This is a 6-12 month journey to sustainable income.

**The Scaling Timeline:**

**Months 1-3: Foundation**
- Build content foundation (20-30 posts)
- Optimize for SEO
- Start building email list
- Establish processes
- $0-$100/month

**Months 4-6: Growth**
- Continue content creation
- Build backlinks
- Improve rankings
- Grow email list
- $100-$500/month

**Months 7-12: Scale**
- Scale content creation
- Target competitive keywords
- Build authority
- Optimize conversions
- $500-$2,000/month

**Year 2+: Optimization**
- Maintain and scale
- Focus on high-value content
- Build authority
- Maximize conversions
- $2,000-$10,000+/month
