# Project 1: Choose Your Niche and Products

## Learning Objective

Choose a profitable affiliate marketing niche and identify 10 products to promote.

## Main Content

This project will walk you through choosing your niche and identifying products to promote. Follow each step in order. Don't skip steps.

**Step 1: Brainstorm Niche Ideas**

**Create List:**

- 10-20 potential niches
- Don't filter yet
- Think broadly
- Include variations

**Sources:**

- Your interests and hobbies
- Problems you've solved
- Industries you know
- Trends you notice
- Competitor analysis

**Step 2: Research Demand**

**For Each Niche:**

**Search Volume:**

- Use Google Keyword Planner
- Check main keywords
- Look for 1,000+ monthly searches
- Check long-tail opportunities

**Community Activity:**

- Reddit subscribers
- Facebook group members
- Forum discussions
- Social media presence

**Questions People Ask:**

- Google "People also ask"
- Quora questions
- Forum posts
- Social media comments

**Step 3: Check Affiliate Opportunities**

**For Each Niche:**

**Amazon:**

- Search category
- Check product availability
- Note prices
- Check ratings

**ClickBank:**

- Browse marketplace
- Check gravity
- Review commissions
- Note products

**Other Programs:**

- ShareASale merchants
- Direct programs
- Niche-specific programs
- Service providers

**Step 4: Analyze Competition**

**For Top 3 Niches:**

**Competitor Analysis:**

- Find top 5 competitors
- Check their content
- Analyze their products
- Assess their strength
- Look for gaps

**Competition Level:**

- Too Easy: No competition = no demand
- Just Right: Some competition = opportunity
- Too Hard: Massive competition = saturated

**Step 5: Choose Your Niche**

**Decision Factors:**

- Interest and ability to learn
- Proven demand
- Affiliate opportunities
- Manageable competition
- Profit potential

**Final Decision:**

- Choose your primary niche
- Have 1-2 backup niches
- Commit to your choice
- Start researching products

**Step 6: Research Products**

**For Your Chosen Niche:**

**Product Categories:**

- What problems does audience have?
- What products solve these problems?
- What do competitors promote?
- What are people searching for?

**Create List:**

- 20-30 potential products
- Different categories
- Various price points
- Mix of programs

**Step 7: Evaluate Products**

**For Each Product:**

**Quality Check:**

- Read reviews
- Check ratings
- Research company
- Look for red flags

**Commission Check:**

- Commission rate
- Cookie duration
- Payment terms
- Minimum payout

**Audience Check:**

- Matches your audience?
- Solves their problems?
- Appropriate price?
- Will they buy it?

**Step 8: Test Products (When Possible)**

**If Possible:**

- Buy and test products
- Use free trials
- Get demos
- Experience products

**Why It Matters:**

- Write honest reviews
- Understand products
- Answer questions
- Build trust

**Step 9: Create Product List**

**Organize Top 10 Products:**

- Product name
- Category
- Commission rate
- Cookie duration
- Price
- Rating/reviews
- Affiliate program
- Notes

**Rank by Priority:**

- High priority (promote first)
- Medium priority (promote later)
- Low priority (backup)

**Step 10: Join Affiliate Programs**

**For Each Product:**

**Join Programs:**

- Amazon Associates
- ClickBank
- ShareASale
- Direct programs
- Other relevant programs

**Get Approved:**

- Complete applications
- Provide required info
- Wait for approval
- Get tracking links

**Step 11: Document Your Research**

**Create Niche Document:**

- Niche description
- Target audience
- Main keywords
- Competitors
- Content ideas
- Products to promote

**Create Product Database:**

- Product details
- Affiliate links
- Commission info
- Promotion notes
- Content ideas

**Using AI Tools:**

**Research:**

- Research niches
- Find products
- Analyze competitors
- Validate demand

**Organization:**

- Create databases
- Organize information
- Generate summaries
- Track research

**Analysis:**

- Analyze niches
- Compare products
- Generate insights
- Make recommendations

**Deliverables:**

**1. Niche Document:**

- [ ] Chosen niche
- [ ] Target audience
- [ ] Main keywords (10-20)
- [ ] Competitors (5)
- [ ] Affiliate programs available
- [ ] Why this niche

**2. Product Database:**

- [ ] 10 products selected
- [ ] Product details
- [ ] Commission rates
- [ ] Affiliate programs
- [ ] Priority ranking
- [ ] Content ideas for each

**3. Affiliate Accounts:**

- [ ] Joined relevant programs
- [ ] Got approved
- [ ] Have tracking links
- [ ] Understand commission structure

## Key Takeaways

- Choose a niche with real demand and affiliate opportunities
- Research thoroughly before committing
- Evaluate products for quality, commissions, and audience fit
- Test products yourself when possible
- Create organized databases for niche and products
- Join affiliate programs and get approved
- Use AI tools to research and organize efficiently
